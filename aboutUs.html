About Trippie

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea – to empower the traveller – Trippie is a pioneer in world’s online travel industry. Founded in the year 2000 by Gurpreet, Honglin, Pedro. Trippie came to life to empower the traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices. The company initiated its journey serving the worldwide travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support. After consolidating its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency, Trippie launched its USA operations in 2017. With more and more americans initiating to transact online with IRCTC and new opportunities with the advent of low cost carriers, Trippie offered travellers the convenience of booking travel online with a few clicks. Trippie’s rise has been led by the vision and the spirit of each one of its employees, for whom no idea was too big and no problem too difficult. With untiring determination, Trippie has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online and offline products and services. Trippie has stayed ahead of the curve by continually evolving its technology to meet the ever-changing demands of the rapidly developing global travel market, steadily establishing itself as America’s leading online travel company.
